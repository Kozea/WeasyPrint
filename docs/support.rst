Support
=======


Professional Support
--------------------

You can improve your experience with CourtBouillon’s tools thanks to our professional
support. You want bugs fixed as soon as possible? Your projects would highly benefit
from some new features? You or your team would like to learn new skills with one of the
technologies we master?

Please contact us by mail_ or by chat_ to get in touch and find the best way we can help
you. We also propose all-in-one `consulting packages`_ for a long-term, priority
support.

.. _mail: mailto:contact@courtbouillon.org
.. _chat: https://matrix.to/#/#CourtBouillon_community:gitter.im
.. _consulting packages: https://weasyprint.org/#packages


Sponsorship
-----------

With `donations and sponsorship`_, you help make the projects better. Donations allow
the CourtBouillon team to have more time dedicated to add new features, fix bugs, and
improve documentation.

.. _donations and sponsorship: https://opencollective.com/courtbouillon
